K2 Firm is a professional services company that has been serving the aerospace & defense vertical since January 2009.

We are a small business, based in Georgia, with a singular focus on customer satisfaction. Product data engineering is our domain expertise.

Product Data Engineering refers to the life cycle management of digital product data. The currency, sufficiency and accuracy of digital product data must be maintained for as long as the weapon system remains in service.

We start with deep domain knowledge and work towards establishing a strategic partnership with customers through consistent delivery of value.

Our staff brings, on average, a decade or more of functional as well as industry expertise.

We can perform against T&M, FFP or CPFF contracts as a prime contractor or a subcontractor. Our employees range from engineers, computer scientists who understand the creation and use of digital product data, software developers, software architects and project managers to product data sustainers who tend to be functional specialists. It is common for our employees to be co-located with customer personnel to maximize value.

Since our founding, we have successfully completed multiple product data engineering projects for the USAF as well as some Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM).
