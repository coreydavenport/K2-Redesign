K2 Firm, LLC Joins Siemens PLM Systems Integrator System Integration Partner Community

Atlanta, Georgia, April 11th, 2013. – K2 Firm, LLC today announced it joined the Siemens PLM Software System Integration partner community, as an alliance partner for the Aerospace & Defense sector in the USA. This alliance allows K2 Firm, LLC to offer its customers the same technology that companies around the world depend on every day to develop some of today’s and tomorrow’s most innovative products.

Siemens PLM Software is a leading global provider of product lifecycle management (PLM) software and services with 7 million licensed seats and more than 71,000 customers worldwide, delivering open solutions to help its customers make smarter decisions that result in better products.

K2 Firm, LLC is a small business providing professional services to the Aerospace & Defense sector. K2 Firm, LLC has been providing professional services to the United States Air Force (USAF) in the general area of configuration management (CM) and product life cycle management (PLM) since 2009. K2 Firm, LLC has assembled a team with domain expertise highly relevant to the past and present PLM/CM modernization efforts within the USAF, as well as proven competencies related to the configuration and customization of the Teamcenter® portfolio, the world’s most widely used digital lifecycle management software. In addition, K2 Firm, LLC has teaming agreements and joint ventures in place to support multiple competitive acquisition modalities.

“K2 Firm, LLC has helped us meet USAF PLM requirements for several years. This partnership with us should equip K2 Firm, LLC to better serve U.S. Air Force needs,” said Barry Nance, Account Executive, Siemens PLM.


“Our partnership with K2 Firm, LLC delivers the combined strength of both our organizations expertise to deliver maximum value to our respective Aerospace & Defense customers,“ said Jim Menego, VP Americas Strategic SI Alliances.
